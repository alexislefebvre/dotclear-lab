Configuration

Since the "Blog Settings" page, you can enable Acronym button on the toolbar writing ticket.
You can also enable the public page of acronyms. Widget "List of acronyms" will be available in the "Presentation widgets" menu.

Utilisation

The acronyms in this list will be automatically recognized by the system when you use the wiki syntax. This means that you will not have to take the entitled, simply enclose an acronym by double question marks.

Example: ??ANSI??

Only acronyms added to this list will be found in the public page of acronyms.
Acronyms Acronym added through the button toolbar writing tickets are not automatically added to this list.

Note: to remove an acronym, just empty its entitled.
