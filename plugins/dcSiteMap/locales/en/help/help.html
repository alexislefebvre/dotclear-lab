How to define Sitemap in the blog interface ?

With a widget:
The most simple is to use the widget "Site map" which proposes a direct link to the dedicated page.

In the template file:
The link to the page "Site map" can also be integrated in the blog interface by editing directly your template file. You just have to add the statement <a href="{{tpl:SiteMapURL}}">{{tpl:lang Sitemap}}</a> at the expected place.
